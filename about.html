About Us

Who We Are

BroaDScale General Merchandise is a trusted local store that provides affordable, practical, and quality everyday products for the community.

We serve students, workers, and families by offering school and office supplies, bags, wallets, beauty products, party items, and household essentials.

Our Mission

To provide affordable daily essentials and convenient shopping for the local community.

Our Vision

To become a reliable and customer-friendly neighborhood store for everyday needs.
